Evest is a mobile coding platform designed by TED Robotics to help visually impaired individuals to learn coding more easily. In this mobile application, trainings in Python, C++, and Java are provided. It is important to remember that not all visual impairments result in complete blindness, and there is sensitivity to colors in certain diseases. It is also important to consider that visually impaired individuals may have difficulty performing daily activities on their own and that Evest was designed with this in mind. We aim to show that with the recent upload of our application to the Google Play Store and App Store, no obstacle can prevent visually impaired individuals from learning to code.

As of Evest launching the WEB version on Google Play and App Store in the near time, we are aiming to display that no obstacle will be enough to cease visually impared indivduals from learning.
