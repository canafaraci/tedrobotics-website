TED Kit is our electronic kit that we developed with ScioVirtual, a non-governmental organization based in the USA. TED Kit combines the accessibility of Arduino with the simplicity of Lego Mindstorm. Our kit is an electronics and coding kit which allows individuals to access STEM education much more comfortably without any discrimination. The goal of TED Kit started with being able to teach Arduino and designing with Fusion 360. After the designs were developed, lesson planning was done with ScioVirtual. The 9-week process, which started with teacher selections, lasted 5 weeks of Java and 4 weeks of Fusion 360 trainings.

  • HC SR04 Ultrsonic Distance Sensor
  • 2 DC Motors
  • 1 Ardunio Uno
  • 1 Breadboard
  • Jumper Cables
  • L298N Motor Driver Board
  • Led Lights
  • Resistanes
  • Potentiometer
